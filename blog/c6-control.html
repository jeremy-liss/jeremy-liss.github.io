When programming, one of my triggers is getting blocked, especially if is a logic problem. I feel like the are too many possibilities and that my brain isn't capable of working out the problem. I can get quite flustered. On reflection it is usually a disproportionate response.

I found the Siberian Northern Railroad exercise interesting, though I found it difficult to fully experience the negative emotion. I think it would be really useful though it may take a bit of practice to be able to do it quickly.
